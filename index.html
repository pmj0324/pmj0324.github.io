Minje Park

Below are the main projects I have worked on in cosmology and astroparticle detector simulation.

  • In cosmology, I develop GENESIS (GENerative AI for thE Simulation and Inference of cosmological fieldS), an optimal-transport flow-matching emulator designed to support astrophysical and cosmological parameter inference.
  • In astroparticle physics, I develop JANUS, a flow-matching model for neutrino event generation and reconstruction for the IceCube Neutrino Observatory. I also worked on OPTICUS, a Vision Transformer model for optical property measurement in the IceCube Upgrade camera system, and contributed to developing a Transformer-based reconstruction model for the next-generation gamma-ray detector SWGO.

Further details are available on the Research and Publications pages.

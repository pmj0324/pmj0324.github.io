GENESIS — Multi-field cosmological simulation and inference

Ongoing

GENESIS is a unified project for multi-field cosmological generation and inference in the CAMELS simulation suite. It learns a conditional flow for dark matter, gas, and temperature fields, and we study training-free likelihood-based inference with the same model.

Generation GENESIS learns a conditional probability-flow vector field with flow matching and integrates it from a simple base distribution to generate dark matter, gas, and temperature maps conditioned on CAMELS parameters.
\[ \frac{d\mathbf{x}_t}{dt} = \mathbf{v}_\theta(\mathbf{x}_t, t, \mathbf{c}) \]
Inference The same learned flow can be evaluated with the change-of-variables formula, giving a training-free route to likelihood-based inference of astrophysical and cosmological parameters.
\[ \log p_\theta(\mathbf{x}_1 \mid \mathbf{c}) = \log p(\mathbf{x}_0) - \int_0^1 \nabla \cdot \mathbf{v}_\theta(\mathbf{x}_t, t, \mathbf{c})\, dt \]

Generated multifield samples from GENESIS.

The manuscript compares GENESIS with reference CAMELS simulations on 2562 maps at 25 h-1 Mpc to assess how well the model reproduces the target fields.

JANUS — Generative AI for IceCube neutrino events

Ongoing
SKKU · Advisor: Prof. Chang Dong Rho · May 2024 – Feb 2026

JANUS explores generative AI for IceCube neutrino event simulation and reconstruction.

The IceCube Neutrino Observatory is a cubic-kilometer detector buried in Antarctic ice, and reconstructing event energy, direction, and vertex from sparse photon signals is a key task.

JANUS studies conditional diffusion and flow-matching models trained on simulated IceCube data to generate detector responses from particle-level conditions and reconstruct event properties from observed signals.

OPTICUS — Vision Transformer for IceCube optical-property measurement

Completed
University of Utah · Advisor: Prof. Carsten Rott · Aug 2023 – Feb 2025

The IceCube Neutrino Observatory is a cubic-kilometer detector buried in Antarctic ice, and OPTICUS uses a Vision Transformer to analyze simulated Upgrade camera images and estimate the optical properties needed for detector calibration.

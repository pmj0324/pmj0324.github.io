GENESIS — Generative Emulator Networks for Inference and Simulation of Cosmological Structure

Ongoing
Computational Cosmology · CAMELS Multifield Dataset · 2025 – Present
GENESIS is a conditional optimal-transport flow-matching emulator that jointly generates dark matter, gas, and gas-temperature fields from the full CAMELS parameter space, including both cosmological and astrophysical feedback parameters. The model uses a U-Net backbone with periodic-boundary convolutions and channel squeeze-and-excitation.
Expand details and materials
🖼 Figure: Multifield samples vs. CAMELS ground truth (Image placeholder)

The manuscript evaluates GENESIS on 2562 maps at 25 h-1 Mpc using auto-power spectra, cross-power spectra, inter-field coherence, one-point distributions, and parameter-response tests across LH, CV, 1P, and EX validation protocols.

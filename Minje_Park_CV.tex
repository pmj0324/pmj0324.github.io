\section{Professional Summary}

\vspace{3pt}

I am an undergraduate researcher in Physics and Electronic \& Electrical Engineering at Sungkyunkwan University. I develop generative AI models, including diffusion models, flow matching, and transformers, for astroparticle physics applications such as detector simulation, neutrino event reconstruction, and cosmological large-scale structure generation. I am a member of the IceCube and SWGO Collaborations, and I have presented this work at domestic and international conferences.
